# Combattant Stats
## Endurance
Endurance is a cost to do actions. When a combattants endurance reaches a threshold it will be prone to breaking. When enough of the troop reach the threshold it will break. If a combattant reaches an even lower theshold it will immediately break
## Formation
Formation is the ability for a troop to maintain a frontline. Formation also defends against gaining the destabilised debuff.
## Block
Block is a units ability to stop strikes. This predominately scales of of the troops shield and armour with smaller benefits for positioning and shield tactics.
## Strike
Strike is a units ability to land blows on an enemy unit. This includes killing the combattant, wounding (debuff that affects formation) or forcing a block.
## Strength
Strength is a units ability to overpower an enemy. This includes their ability to force an enemy back while pushing and the impact of a blocked strike. Additional strength will enable high endurance costs to be spent when doing actions.
